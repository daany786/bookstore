"Caste: The Origins of Our Discontents" by Isabel Wilkerson

"Caste: The Origins of Our Discontents" by Isabel Wilkerson, published in 2020, is a powerful examination of the social structure of caste in the United States and its profound impact on the lives of individuals and communities. Drawing on historical research, real-life narratives, and social analysis, Wilkerson explores the parallels between the American caste system and those of India and Nazi Germany, demonstrating how deeply embedded and enduring caste divisions are in American society.
