"This Changes Everything: Capitalism vs. The Climate" by Naomi Klein

"The Price of Inequality: How Today's Divided Society Endangers Our Future" by Joseph E. Stiglitz, published in 2012, is a critique of the growing income and wealth inequality in the United States. Stiglitz, a Nobel laureate in economics, argues that the increasing disparities in wealth and income not only undermine the social fabric of the country but also pose a threat to its economic and political stability.
