"Evicted: Poverty and Profit in the American City" by Matthew Desmond

"Evicted: Poverty and Profit in the American City" is a nonfiction book written by Matthew Desmond. The book provides a compelling and immersive look into the lives of individuals and families struggling with poverty and housing instability in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Through extensive fieldwork and in-depth interviews, Desmond explores the impact of eviction on the lives of those facing it.
