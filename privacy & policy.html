Privacy & Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how DAMAT collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our website or use our services.
**Information We Collect**
We collect the following types of information from you:
Personal Information: This includes your name, email address, mailing address, phone number, and payment information. We collect this information when you create an account, place an order, or contact us for customer support.
Usage Information This includes information about how you use our website and services, such as the pages you visit, the products you view, and the links you click. We collect this information automatically through cookies and other tracking technologies.
**How We Use Your Information**
We use your information to:
* Process your orders and provide you with the products and services you request. * Send you marketing communications, such as email newsletters and product announcements. * Personalize your experience on our website and services. * Improve our website and services. * Detect and prevent fraud.
**Sharing Your Information**
We share your information with the following third parties:
Payment Processors:** We share your payment information with payment processors to process your payments. Shipping Carriers:** We share your shipping information with shipping carriers to ship your orders. Service Providers:** We share your information with third-party service providers who help us operate our business, such as website hosting providers, email marketing providers, and analytics providers.
**Your Choices**
You have the following choices regarding your information:
Access and Correction: You can access and correct your personal information by logging into your account or contacting us. Marketing Communications: You can unsubscribe from marketing communications by clicking on the "unsubscribe" link in any marketing email you receive. Cookies:** You can disable cookies in your web browser settings. However, disabling cookies may limit your ability to use some of the features of our website and services.
**Data Security**
We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
**Changes to Our Privacy Policy**
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or through a notice on our website.
